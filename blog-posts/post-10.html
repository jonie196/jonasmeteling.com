The Power of nature


After spending a whole day in nature with nothing else but earth, trees, rocks and water I once again realised how powerful, healing and transformative it is to just be with the wildness and rawness of mother earth. It is so simple and yet so rich and potent.

Coming back into civilisation after that intense nature retreat made me feel so strange, I felt the disconnection, loudness and insensitivity of the artificial world we live in. It took me a while to get used to all that again and I immediately missed being in that interconnected field of nature.

This experience showed me again that being in nature is simply natural. Once you open up yourself to truly be in nature with nature you simply feel at home, it feels so natural and obvious to be surrounded by trees, rocks, earth and water. The elements of nature speak to us and are interconnected with us. Our body is over 70% water and we feel a deep oneness and peace that is much harder to feel in our artificial world.

I deeply believe that on a most fundamental level what every human truly longs for is to feel and experience that interconnected field of oneness. We are trying so hard to artificially recreate that feeling that we are missing so deeply and yet it is so simple and so close to us.

For thousands of years our ancestors lived in harmony with nature and knew nothing else but oneness, unity and peace. I can feel this vibration of unity when I tune into the sacred land around me here in northern Canada that was inhabited by native Americans for thousands of years.
