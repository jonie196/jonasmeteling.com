WHY GROUNDING IS SO IMPORTANT ON THE SPIRITUAL PATH




I started my spiritual path by reading books and practicing meditation. I started to learn about deeper aspects of the self and learned how to access deeper states of being. Through meditation my awareness sharpened and my life became more and more transparent. Things started to become less dense and less important because I saw through the illusion.

I went deeper and deeper and explored for many years what reality is made out of and what is true and untrue. This process was extremely transformative and important. It showed me my true self and helped me to let go of the illusory self-identity.

I know many of you are in this process of self dissolution and inquiry into the substance of reality.

After the spiritual opening takes place there needs to be a period of grounding and stabilization. We have to go back into the normality we escaped from before, just this time with a completely different state of being and awareness.
